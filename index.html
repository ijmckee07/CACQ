The home of sport & recreational aviation in South-East Queensland

Famously set between the picturesque Glasshouse Mountains & Moreton Island, Caboolture Aero Club has become the capital of Australian Sport Aviation

Our Club is set in the ideal location of Caboolture, which also has one of the most diverse aviation communities in Australia.

The Airfield

Live to Fly!

Are you interested in becoming a member of the Club at Caboolture Aero Field

On the airfield, you will find flight training and a range of air experiences. You will also find fellow enthusiasts in GA, RAAus, gliding, helicopters, gyrocopters, warbirds and antique aircraft.

The Club

Visit the Airfield

The Caboolture Aero Field is an idyllic place for day trippers. Conveniently located between Brisbane and the Sunshine Coast, there is a fascinating collection of aircraft from World War One to the modern era onsite. Please find our manuals below, or visit our resources section for more.
